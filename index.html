Diversity

Hack In The North has always been pushing the envelope on making developer environment a more inclusive one, especially for women developrers. The last edition of Hack In The North witnessed Diversity sponsors and partners such as Gitlab and Woman Who Code. Further, there were slots for all-girls teams and prizes for best woman team. This spirit of inclusivity, especially after abolition of section 377, is going to see a lot of growth in next edition of Hack In The North. Stay tuned for more updates on our diversty initiatives!
